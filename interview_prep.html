Robotics Interview Prep

A working set of the concepts that come up most often in robotics engineering interviews. Each topic has a short theory recap, a worked example, and an exercise where you fill in a TODO, then run it for real and check the output.

How checking works: "Compile & Check" sends your code to a free public compiler/interpreter service (Wandbox) over the network and compares the program's actual stdout to the expected output. This requires an internet connection · it is a real run, not a text match against your source code.
